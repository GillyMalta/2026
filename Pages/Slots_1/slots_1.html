There are very few rules in the game...

START by clicking the SPIN button and Javascript will randomise your credits for a maximum of 100 credits .
Simultaneously, Javascript will randomise 3 numbers, between 1 and 10 for each slot.

Scoring

  • Numbers between 1 and 9 and worthless however, if you land a 10 in any slot, you will win 1 credit.
  • Land a pair of 10's' in either of the 3 slots, and you will win 10 credits.
  • Land treble 10 's and you will win 100 CREDITS!!
Don't worry about counting 10 's. The table above is keeping track for you!
You also have the option of cashing out... just click the CASH OUT button to retrieve the remaining credits.
Once you burn up all your credits, game will stop, reset and will start over with a new credit limit.
If, at any time, you want to restart the game, simply refresh your browser.

Oh and play responsibly, will ya??!!
